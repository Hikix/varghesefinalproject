Hoot we are

Team 10195, the Night Owls, was formed in 2015 and is currently in their 6th year as part of ECG Robotics, a non-profit organization. In the past year, with new members, we have gained experience in building, programming, CAD designing, marketing and outreach. However, we are always ready to expand our expertise in these fields, learning and growing together as a team.
